Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about QRC Wallet

What makes this wallet quantum-resistant? +
QRC Wallet uses post-quantum cryptographic algorithms approved by NIST, including Dilithium for digital signatures and Kyber for key encapsulation. These algorithms are designed to resist attacks from both classical and quantum computers, ensuring your funds remain secure even as quantum computing advances.
How do I backup my wallet? +
Click on the Security tab and select "Backup Wallet". You'll be shown a 24-word seed phrase. Write this down on paper and store it in a secure location. Never store it digitally or share it with anyone. This seed phrase can restore your wallet on any device.
What happens if I lose my seed phrase? +
If you lose your seed phrase and don't have access to your wallet, your funds will be permanently lost. There is no way to recover a wallet without the seed phrase. This is why it's critical to store multiple copies in secure locations.
Is this wallet really secure? +
Yes. The wallet uses industry-standard security practices including local key storage, encrypted data, and quantum-resistant algorithms. Your private keys never leave your device. However, security also depends on your practices - keep your seed phrase safe and your device secure.
How are transaction fees calculated? +
Transaction fees are 0.2% of the transaction amount with a minimum of 0.01 QRC. These fees go to the network validators and help secure the blockchain. Fees are automatically calculated and shown before you confirm any transaction.
Can I use this wallet on multiple devices? +
Yes! You can import your wallet on any device using your seed phrase. However, for security reasons, we recommend only using your wallet on trusted devices and avoiding public computers.
